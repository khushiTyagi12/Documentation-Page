Noun

A noun is the name of a person,place,animal,thing,human quality/idea/experience/feeling.

Types of noun

  1. Proper Noun
  2. Common Noun
  3. Collective Noun

  4. Possessive Pronoun

  6. Demonstrative adjectives.
  7. Proper adjectives.
